Millennium High School

Millennium High School, Mucherla, Hanmakonda, Warnagal Dist. About Us

In brief...

Millennium High School was founded in the year 2000 and is now being managed by the Pallottine Fathers and Brothers of the Assumption of the Blessed Virgin Mary Province, Bangalore. The school has been striving for excellence in creating tomorrow’s responsible citizens and professionals to lead the future with utmost ease and courage. The lush green 19 acres school campus with separate infrastructure for class room and hostel provide quality education, with special emphasis on discipline and responsibility. The school has strength of 710 students, hailing from different districts of TELENAGANA.

The whole academic year is meticulously planned to meet all curricular, extracurricular and co-curricular activities of the institution. The academic plan is prepared in the beginning of the year to make sure the holistic growth of the child in physical, intellectual, emotional, psychological, cultural and spiritual spheres.
